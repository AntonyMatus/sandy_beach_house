sandy beach coastal group

The Sandy Beach Coastal Group is a legal Mexican Corporation owned by Canadians and based in Playa de Palmeras, Campeche, Mexico. It was established to provide a cost-effective one-stop turnkey approach for all foreigners looking to buy beach front real estate in the state of Campeche. This includes ensuring you have the right legal representation through our partner Ballote and Associates to buy the land through a Fedicomiso or Mexican corporation.
